---

## Overview

Spaces help organize all your project docs by team or topic.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Spaces">
    From the dashboard, click "Spaces" in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create new">
    Click the "+ New Space" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill details">
    Name your Space and add a description. Click "Create".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add members">
    Invite team members if needed.
  </Step>
</Steps>
