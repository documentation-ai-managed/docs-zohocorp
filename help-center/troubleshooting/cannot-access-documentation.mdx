---
title: Cannot Access Documentation
description: Resolve issues accessing Zohocorp documentation.
---

## Symptoms

- Unable to view or edit docs
- "Access Denied" message

## Possible Causes

- Permissions not granted
- Network or login issue

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check permissions">
    Ask your admin to add you to the Space or Folder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify login">
    Ensure you are logged in with the correct Zohocorp account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Log out, clear cache, and log in again.
  </Step>
</Steps>
